# Stories that Lead

## Doctrine

Read [../storyteller-tactics/references/doctrine.md](../storyteller-tactics/references/doctrine.md). Job: **strengthen the team** by sharing struggles, triumphs, and setbacks — culture isn’t left to chance.

## Draft-first

Propose a filled [assets/lead-story-brief.md](assets/lead-story-brief.md) from known team moments. Match density/shape of [assets/exemplar-lead-story-brief.md](assets/exemplar-lead-story-brief.md) (fictional — don’t copy its facts). Mark `TBD`. Ask them to react.

## Process

1. **Curious Tales** — curiosity, caution, flow, envy, sacrifice — what makes people tick. (ch03)
2. **Man in a Hole** — comfort → trigger → crisis → recovery → better place (shared journey). (ch07)
3. **Emotional Dashboard** — highs/lows as story seeds. (ch04)
4. **Thoughtful Failures** — moral in the mistake, no blame theater. (ch04)
5. **Story Bank** — keywords, point, audience, prior tellings, ownership, freshness. (ch09)
6. Map Big/Small × Inside/Outside for where the story lives.

## Output

Filled brief + 1–3 bankable culture stories with metadata.

## Anti-patterns

- Blame-first failure stories
- Only big external heroics; ignoring small inside habits
- Retelling confidential stories in all-hands
