- Recipe stacks live in [chapters/ch02-recipe-cards.md](chapters/ch02-recipe-cards.md) and are implemented as skills

## Core Frameworks (quick)

**Desert Island 7:** Dragon & the City → Story Listening → Trust Me, I'm an Expert → Pitch Perfect → Man in a Hole → Movie Time → Story Bank

**POPP:** Problem (−) → Opportunity (+) → Practical Steps (−) → Promise (+)

**Man in a Hole:** Comfort → Trigger → Crisis → Recovery → Better Place

**No Easy Way:** Problem → Early Success → Setback → Crisis → Recovery → Better Place

**Hero & Guide:** User = Hero; you = Guide with a gift

**Movie Time:** Action + Emotion → Meaning; if no movie in the head, not a story yet

**Three is the Magic Number:** Cap what must stick at three (Attention / Reversal / Goldilocks)
