# Storyteller Pack Doctrine

Compressed operating system for this plugin. Recipe skills enforce it; `storyteller-tactics` holds the deep card reference.

## Roof

**Diagnose the job → run one recipe → draft a story the audience can see.**  
Don’t quiz the user cold. Infer the goal, propose a recipe by name, fill a draft artifact, mark unknowns `TBD`, ask them to react.

## Seven laws

1. **Job before craft.** Sell, motivate, convince, connect, explain, lead, or impress — pick one. Style without a job is polish on fog.
2. **Audience before pitch.** Who they are, what they fear, what they hope — or the story is about you, not them.
3. **User is Hero; you are Guide.** Spotlight on their journey; your gift is the capability that helps.
4. **Trust is character under cost.** Credentials without values-in-action stories fail Pitch Perfect.
5. **Arcs beat lists.** POPP, Man in a Hole, No Easy Way, Rags to Riches — named beats carry memory better than bullet features.
6. **Movie-check everything.** If no scene plays in the head, it’s not a story yet (Movie Time). Cap what must stick at **three**.
7. **Permission and provenance.** Bank whose story it is, who heard it, whether it’s stale. Never steal or gossip.

## Stack

```text
Goal / job (recipe)
        ↓
Audience Profile (+ Innovation Curve if “new” is risky)
        ↓
Explore / Character inputs (listening, trust, motivation)
        ↓
Structure arc (Hole / Rags / No Easy Way / Voyage…)
        ↓
Function delivery (Pitch Perfect, sales story, conversation)
        ↓
Style pass (Movie Time, Threes, Show & Tell, Cut to the Chase)
        ↓
Organise (Story Bank metadata + where Big/Small × Inside/Outside)
```

## Draft-first rule

Propose a filled story artifact from available context. Ask the user to react — **don’t** open with the seven “Do you know…?” questions as a blank interview. Use that diagnostic *internally* when the job is unclear.

## Hard nos

- Feature dump dressed as a story
- Making yourself the Hero of the customer’s journey
- Clickbait secrets with no payoff
- Only Rags to Riches for sceptical buyers (use No Easy Way / Pride & Fall)
- Retelling without permission
