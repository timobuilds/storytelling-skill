# Stories that Explain

## Doctrine

Read [../storyteller-tactics/references/doctrine.md](../storyteller-tactics/references/doctrine.md). Job: **make strategy matter in the real world** — not a dusty deck.

## Draft-first

Propose a filled [assets/explain-story-brief.md](assets/explain-story-brief.md). Match density/shape of [assets/exemplar-explain-story-brief.md](assets/exemplar-explain-story-brief.md) (fictional — don’t copy its facts). Mark `TBD`. Ask them to react.

## Process

1. **Order & Chaos** — Known vs Unknown; are you imposing order or disrupting rigidity? (ch03)
2. **Good & Evil** — which moral foundation / two-rights tension. (ch03)
3. **What’s It About?** — change / new information / personal benefit one-liner. (ch05)
4. **Rolls Royce Moment** — one vivid exemplary relatable detail. (ch08)
5. **Story Hooks** — unexpected or knowledge hooks (deliver on the promise). (ch08)
6. Optional arc wrap with Five Ts or Man in a Hole.

## Output

Filled brief + a one-page strategy story (hook → stake → moment → ask).

## Anti-patterns

- Adjective salad with no Order/Chaos map
- Clickbait hooks with no payoff
- Strategy as spreadsheet with no Rolls Royce moment
