# Stories that Motivate

## Doctrine

Read [../storyteller-tactics/references/doctrine.md](../storyteller-tactics/references/doctrine.md). Job: **get people behind you** — plan alone isn’t enough; show what’s driving you and the realistic journey.

## Draft-first

Propose a filled [assets/motivation-story-brief.md](assets/motivation-story-brief.md). Match density/shape of [assets/exemplar-motivation-story-brief.md](assets/exemplar-motivation-story-brief.md) (fictional — don’t copy its facts). Mark `TBD`. Ask them to react.

## Process

1. **Dragon & the City** — City (status quo), Dragon (threat + opportunity), Escape / Defend / Attack. (ch03)
2. **Drive Stories** — autonomy, mastery, purpose (yours and theirs). (ch05)
3. **Three Great Conflicts** — Nature / Society / Self; put the greatest fight at the heart. (ch03)
4. **Innovation Curve** — match story to pioneer → traditional risk thermostat. (ch07)
5. **No Easy Way** — Problem → Early Success → Setback → Crisis → Recovery → Better Place. (ch07)
6. Style pass + Story Bank metadata.

## Output

Filled motivation brief + a 60–90s spoken narrative using No Easy Way beats.

## Anti-patterns

- Vision without Dragon/City stakes
- Ignoring sceptics’ Pride & Fall soundtrack
- One story for every adopter type
